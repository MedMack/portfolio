The “Mozilla Tunisia v2” Project

Mozilla Tunisia is the local community representing the Mozilla Foundation in Tunisia. As a part of the community and the coding team espcially, we started working on a new and better update of our website.

If you're interested in joining the community, check our website.

The inspiration

For more than 2 years, a project called “Mozilla Tunisia v2” was just and idea with some sketches on paper. I was following some of my friend Sofien’s work with the Community Building Team (known as Participation nowadays) trying to rethink structures and compositions — which I found amazing — to evolve and fix the community problems. I still remember sharing the very first draft hesitating but was surprised seeing him liking the idea and suggesting to share it with the CBT and communities. You can check the amazing work he did here.

The idea was a total new rework of the community structure, scalable with more flexibility and less roles with “making the community go big” as a primary goal. The first draft was almost a perfect fit for our team, putting leaders a central position to manage the community activities while the technical and logistical contributors together for a better/easier coordination and communication.

My Role

As the lead designer of the community, my job was to work on basically on the UI refresh but also on the how I can translate my team's ideas into a design. Sounds easy doesn't it ?

The new - contribute feature

If you ask anyone from the community which question he heard the most, the reply will be "How can I join your community ?". In order to tackle this problem, we came up with the /contribute page where people can fill a small form and pick the domain where they would like to contribute.
